STATUS OF EXPORT OF INDUSTRIAL PRODUCTS IN THE TEN MONTHS OF 2020.
Exports of domestic products in the first ten months of 2020 amounted to 1253.1 million US dollars, which is 291.8 million US dollars or 30.4 percent more than the same period last year, while exports of industrial products amounted to 1184.8 million US dollars. Compared to the same period last year, an increase of 37.9 percent or 325.6 million US dollars. should be noted that in the first ten months of 2020 a number of industrial products were exported, the export of which has not been previously registered. For example, antiseptic materials (ethyl alcohol) were exported in the amount of 189.0 thousand US dollars or 117.4 tons, medical masks and other medical stuff materials - 1472.4 thousand units. Also during this period the diversification of production of juices and fruit and vegetable juices has been established, more than 10 new types, including: canned sweet corn, tomato and cherry juices, juices without added sugar, juices with a mixture of tropical fruits, etc. were exported for the first time.

Promotion of domestic products of Tajikistan through Geographical Indications - reinforcing the relationship between people, place and products.
A national technical working group was established under the Government's Export Agency Of the Republic of Tajikistan for the determination of geographical indications of products. This working group was created at the initiative of the Export Agency, relevant ministries and departments, the private sector and international industry organizations in accordance with the instruction of the Government of the Republic of Tajikistan. The first meeting of this working group on April 28, 2020 was opened with opening remarks by the Director Export Agencies. In his speech, he noted that the Government of the Republic of Tajikistan pays special attention to product promotion and strengthening of the country's export potential, and therefore supported the initiative to establish a National Technical Working Group on Export Promotion and Identification geographical indications. During the meeting, Rakhimzoda Bahodur, Deputy Director of the Export Agency, informed the participants about the main goals and objectives of the Working Group and said that its members should first develop a list of potential products and discuss it with representatives from groups and industry associations. He added that representatives of the scientific community, including industry scientists, should be involved in the process of determining the specificity and special quality characteristics of the selected products with the purpose of preparing the evidence base for the development of their feasibility study. Participants discussed and supported the form, potential products and future activities of the Working Group within the CANDY-V project, which is being implemented by the European Union program in Central Asia (stage 5). AT currently, weekly training courses are held in the form of webinars with the participation of foreign experts. The Export Agency under the Government of the Republic of Tajikistan will carry out activities this Working Group and ongoing activities involving a number of representatives from relevant ministries and departments, the private sector (manufacturers, processors, exporters, industry associations, etc.).

Trade and economic cooperation between the Republic of Tajikistan and China People's Republic
In order to promote and export agricultural products including lemons, cherries, honey, grapes and fruit, On December 27, 2019, a meeting of entrepreneurs of the Republic of Tajikistan was organized with the participation of 30 entrepreneurs from Xinjiang Autonomous Region of the People's Republic of China. The collaboration was signed.

The purpose of this meeting is to promote trade and economic cooperation between the Republic Tajikistan and The People's Republic of China in order to promote exports, as well as familiarization with the production and export potential of Tajikistan.

They noted that organizing and holding such meetings will provide the parties with up-to-date information. about the best world practices of promoting international trade, export of goods and products and to establish mutually beneficial relationship.

During the meeting, an exchange of views took place on the closest neighbors and strategic partners of the Republic Tajikistan.

At the same time, they noted that our countries are connected by mountains and rivers, since the friendship of our peoples comes from the distant past. Even 2000 years ago, the Great Silk Road united not only our countries, but also the starting point of friendly relations between our peoples.

Today, relations between China and Tajikistan are developing dynamically.
